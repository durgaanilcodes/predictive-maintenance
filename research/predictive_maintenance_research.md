# Predictive Maintenance Research

## Introduction
Predictive Maintenance is a technique that uses data analysis, machine learning, and sensor information to predict equipment failures before they occur.

## Importance
- Reduces unexpected breakdowns
- Lowers maintenance costs
- Improves equipment lifespan
- Increases operational efficiency

## Role of IoT Sensors
IoT sensors collect real-time data such as:
- Temperature
- Vibration
- Pressure
- Current

## Role of Machine Learning
Machine learning models analyze sensor patterns and predict possible failures in advance.

## Applications
- Manufacturing industries
- Automotive sector
- Smart factories
- Fleet management
